Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I record a drive? — Tap the + tab, then "Start Drive". Tarmac uses background GPS so you can lock your phone while driving.
  • Can I delete my account? — Yes. Go to Profile → Edit Profile → Delete Account. All your data is permanently removed.
  • How do I restore my Pro subscription? — Open any Pro feature prompt → Paywall → Restore Purchases.
  • My drive didn't upload. — Check your internet connection. Pending drives upload automatically when you're back online.
  • How do I report a user or content? — Tap the three-dot menu on any drive, post, or profile and select "Report".
  • Can I export my route? — Tarmac Pro subscribers can export drives as GPX or KML files from the drive detail screen.
